Avimir
Own safety.

All modern mobile phones have one very weak spot - security - namely, passwords. Of course, you can try to enter long given a 16 -letter passwords on a small screen, using a touch-screen keyboard. But all the inconveniences immediately obvious: it is necessary to spend a lot of time , similar to a password is very easy to spy and very difficult to remember . Our friend and partner of the United States came up with a very simple and fundamental concept . And what if the password is not text and picture ... or music , or your position on the map. In general, any audio and visual information. Plus, what if he can change the password every time you run , making it virtually impossible to fit. This idea and called "dynamic audiovisual password", or short - Avimir. And this was only the beginning . Avimir'a main advantage that it and its own unique for each user.

I, for example , can create your avimir of 10 random images and me to log on, you will need to choose two with the dog. Someone more like avimir, where you need to get into the rhythm of your favorite song , while playing on the background 3 more unfamiliar tunes. Someone wants to be avimir open automatically every time he comes home. But the possibilities do not end there . Avimir - is a complete tool for marketing and brand awareness . Brand can create a personal account for each user , using avimir: the bank may be your avimir, while the supermarket chain - its. Moreover, all our avimir'y are designed to be backward compatible with conventional passwords , all the mechanics and mathematics to transform takes on our program . Avimir - this is the first personal protection.

Kolight
Confident step.

After the success idbm, we began to think about expanding the range of marketing devices. And during a visit to one of the shows we noticed interactive floor . Projector , motion sensor and a game of football - so look in those days , such a system . The idea was shocking and we liked it very much , but the execution made ​​itself felt . That's when we decided to raise the bar on technology in this area and began work on a new system - kolight. The first step was to develop a system of pattern recognition and motion. In those days, the technology in this area were quite weak - we had a lot of things to "invent ". We worked a lot of options both hardware and mathematically - algorithmic part . We have already had experience with artificial intelligence , but it was our first project in the field of computer vision . Initially, we set out to make the device as compact and portable , so we were limited in computational resources . We had to use the incredible ways to optimize our algorithms and , even , parallel computing technology , peeped during the development of high- loaded websites.

During the existence of the platform and the core of the system have been about a dozen steps , together with the progress and discoveries in the field of computer vision and increase the computing power of processors. Kolight and still is at the peak of progress - a branch of artificial vision is one of the research priorities for most modern universities. Next, we set about creating interactive effects , and then it was the sore spot of such systems. Effects often corresponded to a specific task and did not allow a flexible manipulation of content : everything that could be done - it change the background to another image . And then our system shone : it permitted to create full effects using only a mouse. The user can completely change the atmosphere and branding each of the fifty patterns of effects throughout the entire 10 minutes , and create your own - after just one week of training. In addition, the extended system used Pro professional graphics and physical packages in order to give access to the super- modern and realistic content. Naturally, the system can be synchronized via the Internet, to stitch images into a huge canvas to work with video support augmented reality - all possibilities are countless. Kolight became confident step in a new direction of the company.

Medical Pro
New medical handwriting.

Medicine has always been a conservative industry. With access to a huge budget and one of the largest arsenals of technology , most doctors still prefer to use paper and pen . We never liked it and we , together with our partners , we decided to change it . So , a system of MedicalPro. Of course, all the standard features in CRM and ERP systems have been implemented , with particular diligence. But the emphasis was placed on the following - the system allows you to integrate and synchronize many disparate medical devices , read each of them , and most importantly - collect them in a single window in which any qualified doctor will be instantly visible status of the patient. Inside the system was available card histories , prescribed and taken medications surveys contraindications, diagnoses , and even graphic information , such as X-rays. The system also takes on the part of administrative responsibility : she was able to work with schedules and visits , distribute offices, monitor the workload of staff to distribute the survey to monitor the delivery of drugs and more. Communication with the doctor has become much easier .

WitBoard
Hand Innovations.

The onscreen keyboard has always been the weakest point of any touch-screen device. She actually tries to repeat a mechanical device , created 100 years ago, which should always lie horizontally on the table , and specifically designed for the trained user dials " medium text" all 10yu fingers. When it comes to entering text , any portable device almost immediately becomes unusable . Not to mention the fact that the current SMS - this is not the average text 100 years ago. Such problems are solved by system WitBoard. Fully -screen "keyboard " adapts to a particular person , his style and print texts, which he usually enters . The system performs statistical analysis of the texts , trying to predict the next letter entered and transforms this knowledge into a screen prompt to the user. View tips depends on the device : it can be a path , moving away , or even rain letters familiar keyboard . The result is a new way to enter text . Entering could not be specifically designed for children or people with disabilities. Classical keyboard was no longer a mandatory attribute , because one could easily enter your text into the phone. Ironically, innovation sometimes literally in the hands of the developer.

Les Express
Most comfortable industrial programm.

Wood , to put it the necessary properties before use for furniture or construction to dry moisture . And it's not reached the 10 minute task , and a very complex industrial process, requiring the intervention of the Institute with a long experience of research. On how, where, how long and with what parameters you just dry wood depends on what you can make of it , the color , the quality and flexibility of future products. Our task - to broadcast library research and mathematical calculations into something that people can use , with little or no computer experience . The system had to understand and work with more than a dozen parameters simultaneously : she must have known about the type of wood , 3 different kinds of humidity, temperature 8 types , the exact time of day, time of drying and much more. It was also necessary to ensure complete stability and autonomy of the system. Each run of the program had a solid budget and failures were unacceptable . When problems have been solved , we went to innovate. The weakest point of the modern industrial system - the user interface . While working with them the feeling that control a spaceship - a huge number of buttons, lights and circuit breakers . And then stepped in our experience . We have created a system where you can easily enjoy all the drying modes , to begin the process in 3 mouse click , and create their own profiles , which give a special quality of materials and color. We believe that brought some aesthetic culture in most industries.
