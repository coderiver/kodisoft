Internships

From idea to product, through all the details and without compromise. The whole cycle of the product from sketch to own software and hardware with an excellent design.
Software
Software - a link between the "iron" and man. As a trainee - software developer you have to turn abstract concepts in the form of long codes, algorithms and data structures in a real product. Using all their knowledge to create applications and software, which will be admired.
Hardware
You will be able to apply their knowledge in electronics and electrical engineering, creating a foundation of technical products. Along with professional growth, you have the opportunity to turn their layout design to the finished product. Iron - it's always hard, but from what will be the "hardware" depends on what product we get the output.
Design
There is no greater pleasure than to see how your finished 3D models are transformed into colorful animation or body of finished products. Designers play a crucial role in creating visually attractive applications and interfaces, product appearance as well as its further advancement. As an intern designer you have to work on projects, creating beautiful interfaces for future prototypes of interactive animation, industrial design product.
Since the first internship in the summer of 2008, it became an integral part of our company and our team. During this time we tried a variety of formats and destinations internships, perfecting and honing their own skills in education interns, and selection of the best in our team. Only the principles of training remain unchanged - "Win - Win" and "Co Creation" for a period of two month.
