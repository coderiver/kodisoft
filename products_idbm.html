IDBM
Intellect Device Bluetooth Marketing.

The world's first smart device for Bluetooth- marketing. Our device enhances the mobile marketing and implements an entirely new form of communication with consumers. Bluetooth- marketing allows you to transfer free of any electronic information (advertising images, music , programs, news reports , etc.) on your mobile phone , PDA , laptop or other device equipped with a Bluetooth-module , as well as to receive a response from the recipient .

All our units are equipped with technology «KoSmartDetect», which allows the most sophisticated and high-quality stocks and Bluetooth Bluetooth distribution , as well as to build large-scale advertising network. Hardware-based company Kodisoft already built a large number of networks worldwide.

This is the technology of mobile advertising the new generation , the only device that supports Bluetooth 4.0, monitoring, management , statistics, order management system , and customers - and all this in the most convenient on-line management system marketing tools - Kodisoft Marketing Portal.

IDBM - this is the only device for bluetooth marketing, whose effective radius distribution is 95 meters (300 feet optional) . IDBM - more than just a delivery - it is also Wi-Fi and Bluetooth access point to the Internet, radio broadcast in the FM-band , reception and service orders, ticket booking, etc.
